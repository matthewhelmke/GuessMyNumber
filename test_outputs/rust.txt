
Welcome to Guess My Number!

The computer will select a random whole number between 1 and 100. Your goal is to guess that number. You will get a turn, then a computer player will get a turn. Each of you are aware of the other's guesses. The first one to guess the number correctly will win. Try to guess in as few turns as possible.
